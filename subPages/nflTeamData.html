# Weeks To Analyze:



* Relative statistics adjust given the actual opponents over the given time horizon. For example, assume the Patriots scored 100, 110, and 120 total fantasy points over the last three weeks. But their opponents over that same period (ignoring games where they played the Patriots) give up 130, 80, and 100 points on average. Therefore the Patriots relative total fantasy points would be (100 - 130 + 110 - 80 + 100 - 120)/3 = -6.67 implying they scored roughly 7 points less on average over the last three weeks then their opponent would normally permit. This statistic attempts to adjust for schedule in case a team had a easy/hard schedule over a given span.
